# Course Tracking Template

## Google Sheets Setup Instructions

1. **Access Google Sheets**: Go to [Google Sheets](https://sheets.google.com).
2. **Create a New Spreadsheet**: Click on the `Blank` option to create a new spreadsheet.
3. **Set Up the Spreadsheet**:  
   - Label the columns as follows: `Course Name`, `Start Date`, `End Date`, `Status`, `Notes`.
   - Adjust the column widths for better visibility.
4. **Input Your Courses**: Fill in the rows with your courses, including relevant details for each course.
5. **Apply Conditional Formatting**:  
   - Highlight your status cells and go to Format > Conditional formatting.  
   - Set rules to color-code based on the status (e.g., Completed, Ongoing, Not Started).
6. **Share Your Spreadsheet**: If you want to collaborate, click on `Share` in the top right corner and add email addresses of your collaborators.

## Template Overview
You can use this template to log your courses, track progress, and keep notes on your learning journey. Stay organized and make sure you never miss a deadline!
